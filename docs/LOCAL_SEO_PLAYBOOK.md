# Local SEO Playbook

Google Business Profile (GBP) setup and maintenance for TX and FL service areas.

---

## GBP Setup

### Service Area Business (SAB)

- Create separate GBP listings for each region (TX and FL)
- Use **Service Area Business** — do not show a physical address
- Define service areas by cities/regions served

### Categories

**Primary:**
- Dumpster Rental
- Waste Container Supplier
- Roll-Off Container Supplier

**Secondary (as applicable):**
- Construction Waste Disposal
- Junk Removal Service

### Services

List concrete services (no "coming soon"):

- Roll-off dumpster rental
- Dump trailer rental
- Construction debris removal
- Residential dumpster rental
- Commercial dumpster rental
- Same-day delivery (if offered)
- Junk removal (if offered)

---

## Weekly Posting Checklist

| Task | Frequency |
|------|-----------|
| New GBP post (offer, update, event) | 1–2× per week |
| Respond to all reviews | Within 24–48 hours |
| Update services/hours if changed | As needed |
| Add new photos | 1× per month |
| Check Q&A for new questions | Weekly |

---

## Review Workflow

1. **Monitor:** Enable GBP notifications for new reviews
2. **Respond:** Reply within 24–48 hours
3. **Tone:** Professional, grateful, solution-oriented
4. **Negative reviews:** Acknowledge, apologize, offer to resolve offline
5. **No "coming soon":** Only mention services you actually offer

---

## Regional Focus

- **TX:** North Houston, Spring, surrounding areas
- **FL:** Brevard County and surrounding areas

Optimize GBP descriptions and posts with local keywords and city names.
